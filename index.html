cincinati brewery, pizza, and event center

Little Miami Brewing Company is a Cincinnati Brewery nestled in the heart of historic Old Milford, Ohio, on the banks of the scenic Little Miami River.
You'll love this Cincinnati brewery, where passion for craft beer meets good times and great food.

CRAFT BEER

Since 2017, we've been dedicated to brewing award winning small-batch craft beer, made with fresh, local ingredients whenever possible. Explore our ever-changing lineup of brews, from hoppy IPAs to crisp lagers, and everything in between. Find what's on tap at the finest

Find What's On Tap>>
DELICIOUS FOOD

Our kitchen dishes up mouth-watering pizzas, salads, pretzels, chili, and nachos, perfect for pairing with your favorite beer. Check out our menu and discover why we are the go-to brewery in Cincinnati

View Our Menu>>
VIBRANT TAPROOM

Pull up a seat in our inviting taproom, where you can relax with friends, catch a Bengals or Reds game on TV, or enjoy live music on select nights. Little Miami Brewing Company is the perfect Cincinnati brewery for an unforgettable night out.

SCENIC ROOFTOP

Planning a celebration? Our spacious Event Center can accommodate weddings, concerts, rehearsals dinners, birthday parties, retirement parties, and more. Host your event at Little Miami Brewing Company, the best brewery in Cincinnati
Whether you're a craft beer enthusiast, a foodie, or simply looking for a great place to gather with friends, we invite you to experience the Little Miami Brewing Company difference. Cheers!
